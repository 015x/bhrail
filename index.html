BRIANRAIL

Consultant for rail

An experienced railway professional leading vision, strategy, operational planning, and tactical delivery of national and route level functions for Network Rail and train operators for 18 years. Generated and lead collaboration with industry stakeholders and government to improve performance, safety, and resilience, delivering a safer railway for passengers and freight users.

Key achievements

Strategic Leadership

Developed and directed the rail industry five-year weather strategy in collaboration with DfT and the Cabinet Office. Built a first in class assurance management maturity model adopted by all NR Routes and Regions. Managed and deployed assurance framework (GB Rail Approach Documents) to prepare for and manage extreme weather across the industry.

Operational Excellence

Created and deployed new National Operating Procedures and Industry Standards. Created operational controls for the national risk register for weather and climate. Developed and led the national weather forecasting contract for the rail industry.

Expert Advisory

Led as subject matter expert on train crash investigations, developed and deployed recommendations at industry level. Built relationships with key industry bodies including RSSB, RAIB, DfT, and ORR.
